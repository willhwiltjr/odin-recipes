Garlic Roasted Chicken

"Chicken again? at least its not fish wife-2018"

Ingredients

  • four chicken thighs bone in skin on
  • 1Tbsp salt
  • 1Tbsp pepper
  • 1Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1 pinch cayanne powder
  • 5 Tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees F placing a foil lined pan into the heating stove
  2. mix seasonings in a bowl and reserve 1Tbsp olive oil for pan
  3. pull skin back exposing meat while still leaving skin attached
  4. using no more than 1 Tbsp of oil per thigh apply a light coating of oil to the bottom skinless portion as well as the tops of the pulled back skin
  5. apply a light coating of seasoning mixture to exposed meat on thighs then replace skin dust tops of skins with leftover spice mixture
  6. apply leftover olive oil to roasting pan with brush and place chicken thighs on pan and cook until an internal temp of 175 degrees F
  7. let chicken thighs rest for 5 minutes before plating
