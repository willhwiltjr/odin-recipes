Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne noodles
  • 4 cups mozzerella
  • 2 cups ricotta
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 pound itialian sausage
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 Tbsp tomatoe paste
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning

Steps

  1. shred cheese, dice onion, mince garlic, chop parsely, and beat egg
  2. preheat oven to 350 degrees F and cook pasta sheets to al dente placing in a bowl of icewater after cooking to retard the cooking process
  3. start browning ground beef and sausage in a pan on meduim heat then in same pan after a minute add diced onion until it gets some color then add garlic
  4. once meat is brown and onions are carmelized drain fat and add pasta sauce tomatoe paste and itilain seasoning and let simmer for 5 minutes then place in container
  5. in a seperate container combine 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzerella, 1/4 cup shredded parmesan, ricotta, egg, and parsley
  6. in a 9"x13" pan add a cup of meat sauce folowed by a layer of noodles followed by a 1/3 cup of cheese mixture repeat until out of materials then cover with foil
  7. bake covered for 45 minutes
  8. uncover and top with remaining shredded cheeses then bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes until top is brown and bubbly
  9. take out of oven and rest for a minimum of 15 minutes resting helps keep the slices together when cutting once done resting cut into squares and serve
